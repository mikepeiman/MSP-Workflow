A Web Development Workflow

I’m stoked you want to learn web development!

My name is Mike Peiman. I built this thing to help empower you to master your creative and economic destiny by learning the art and science of web development.

To master web development, you need the right tools, and you need the right workflow. This guide will walk you through the setup of your web development environment on your Windows pc, and the process of using the tools of the trade. We'll really be starting from scratch, with the assumption of a brand new, stock computer with no development environment setup yet.
